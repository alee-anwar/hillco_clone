What We Do

HillCo, LLC is a bee equipment supplier committed to helping beekeepers maintain their colonies and harvest delicious honey with our line of quality products.

We carry a variety of beekeeping equipment, including beehives and components, bee suits, smokers, uncapping tools, uncapping tanks, honey tanks, and especially our signature honey extractors.

We also work with reputable bee breeders to supply you with healthy bees and queens and supplies to take care of them. (Note: Live bees are available only for pickup at our facility in Minonk, IL.)

Contact us today with any questions you may have about beekeeping or what we can provide as a bee equipment supplier.
